Prince Emmanuel Sanitarium & Clinic

Let food be your medicine and your medicine be food

We are PRINCE EMMANUEL CLINIC AND SANITARIUM. The hospital was established in the year 2005.

Mission: Is to give quality natural health care to all both in Ghana and the diaspora. Our vision is to help as many as possible to reform their eating habits and to change people's lifestyles. We give health counseling to our clients who visit our facility.

Motto: "Let food be your medicine and your medicine be food". Eating a plant-based diet heal diseases as junk food is the cause of all ailments. The world is battling in vain with chronic diseases. The solution is change of diet and lifestyle. And that is what we are practicing.

We have an uncountable number of patients who have been healed who have been given up by Orthodox hospitals. We have several people who have come from overseas to receive healing. By God's grace, we have reversed several cases of diabetes heart disease cancer stroke kidney other chronic diseases with diet and lifestyle changes.

Currently, we have branches in Accra, Tema, and Tarkwa. Kumasi being our headquarters.
